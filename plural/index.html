Madison Rye Progress is plural

As a median system, my identity comprises several different aspects, each of which is named, and each of which informs the ways in which I act, speak, and otherwise move through the world. Given my furry identity, these tend to take the form of anthropomorphic animals. Most of their names are cribbed from the Post-Self Cycle, a set of four novels I wrote from 2020 to 2022. You may click on them for more information. Given the role-play and fictive nature, much of the information has noemata/lore mixed in.
